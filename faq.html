What is Raindrop Technique?

The Raindrop Technique is a method of using a combination of reflexology, aromatherapy, massage techniques, and essential oils applied on your spine and on the feet. High quality essential oils are placed on the reflex points on the feet where the spine is represented. The Raindrop Technique is designed to bring balance to the body with its relaxing and mild application. The Raindrop Technique is a gentle application of essential oils and massage without using hard pressure or trying to force the body to change, which should never be done.

What should I expect during session and preparation for treatment?

Prior to a Raindrop Therapy Session please hydrate and dress in comfortable clothes you do not mind getting oily - or bring a change of clothes for after your session. For the session, you will undress down to your underwear but if you prefer, you can wear a loose fitting or stretchy pant and keep them on. We also ask that you remove any jewellery, the only exception is rings that you never take off. You will lie on our table, face up prior to the practitioner entering the room, and remain covered throughout the session. The essential oils are applied in a specific technique on both the feet and spine; hot towel compresses will follow. The process takes about 60 minutes, after which we recommend you rest for a few minutes while doing some deep breathing exercises before getting dressed. Rest and hydration are also recommended following your treatment.
