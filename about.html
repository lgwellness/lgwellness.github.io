I am Lonni Gallagher..

I am a dedicated and passionate colon hydrotherapist, embarked on her journey in this field in 2022. Originating from the vibrant city of South Florida, she relocated to New York to pursue her calling in the realm of colon hydrotherapy. With an unwavering commitment to providing exceptional care and promoting holistic well-being, Lonni attended Cathy Shea School for Colon Hydrotherapy in Juno Beach, Florida. Driven by a understanding of the profound impact colon hydrotherapy can have on an individual's overall health, Lonni is dedicated to providing a safe, professional, and compassionate environment for her clients.

Her expertise lies in helping individuals achieve optimal intestinal health, relieve digestive discomfort, and enhance their overall well-being through the practice of colon hydrotherapy. With a focus on patient education and empowerment, Lonni strives to create a positive and transformative experience for each client. She firmly believes that a healthy colon is a cornerstone of overall wellness and vitality, and she is committed to guiding her clients on their journey toward achieving and maintaining optimal gut health.

Lonni Gallagher's unwavering dedication to excellence, coupled with her extensive training and experience, positions her as a sought-after colon hydrotherapist in Albany, NY. She is committed to staying abreast of the latest advancements in colon hydrotherapy and is actively involved in ongoing continuing education to provide her clients with the most cutting-edge and effective treatments available.
